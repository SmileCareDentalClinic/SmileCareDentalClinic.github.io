About the Dentist

Dr. Dhaval Shah, B.D.S

With over 15 years of clinical and teaching experience, Dr. Dhaval Shah brings advanced dental expertise and compassionate care to Smile Dental Clinic. A BDS graduate from the renowned Government Dental College & Hospital, Ahmedabad, Dr. Shah has trained extensively in orthodontics and has served as a senior dental tutor at Shree Krishna Hospital, Karamsad, for over a decade. Known for his precision, ethical practice, and patient-first approach, Dr. Shah is committed to delivering personalized, high-quality dental care.

Dr. Krishna Patel, B.D.S

Dr. Krishna Patel is a trusted dental professional with over 7 years of experience in delivering personalized, high-quality dental care. A BDS graduate from Narsinhbhai Patel Dental College, Visnagar, she specializes in preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry. Known for her gentle touch and patient-focused approach, Dr. Patel is committed to making every visit comfortable while helping patients achieve a healthy, confident smile. She stays updated with the latest advancements to ensure the best care at Smile Dental Clinic.
